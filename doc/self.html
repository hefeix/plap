Self-Reference, Self-Improvement, and Invariants

AIXI and AIXItl are an idealized models of "optimal intelligence" based on program induction. An important limmitation of this paradigm is that AIXItl cannot model e.g. its circuits being scrambled - the internals of operation are behind an opaque curtain with the rest of the universe on the other side (thank to Eliezer Yudkowsky for this insight).
